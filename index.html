IoT Intrusion Detection

The proliferation of Internet of Things (IoT) devices has ushered in a new era of technological ubiquity enhancing convenience and efficiency across a wide array of applications. From smart home appliances to autonomous vehicles and military-grade drones, these devices provide seamless real-time access to information, revolutionizing data collection and processing. However, this technological paradigm also introduces vulnerabilities to cyber threats and this surge in connectivity to a burgeoning wave of cyber threats particularly from malicious actors seeking unauthorized access to sensitive information.
